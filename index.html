Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our tinting services? We’ve got answers!

What types of tints do you offer?

We offer premium Solrex, Anda, and 3M tints, including frosted films for office privacy. Each brand has options for different light transmission (VLT), UV protection, and heat rejection levels to suit your needs.

How does your free home service work?

Our expert team brings all necessary tools and materials to your location—home, office, or elsewhere—for a hassle-free tint installation. Just schedule a time, and we’ll handle the rest at no extra cost!

Are your tints covered by a warranty?

Yes, all our tints (Solrex, Anda, and 3M) come with manufacturer-backed warranties covering issues like peeling, bubbling, or fading. Contact us for specific warranty details based on your chosen tint.

How long does the tinting process take?

Installation time varies by project size. A typical car takes 2–4 hours, while residential or commercial jobs may take 4–8 hours. We’ll provide an estimate during your free quote.

Can frosted tints be used for privacy in offices?

Absolutely! Our 3M frosted films are perfect for adding privacy and a sleek look to office spaces while still allowing natural light. They’re customizable for patterns or logos too!
